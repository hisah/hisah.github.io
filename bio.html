Short Biography of Dr. Haruna Isah

Haruna Isah is a SOSCIP Postdoctoral Fellow at the Big-Data Analytics and Management Laboratory (BAM Lab) of the School of Computing at Queen’s University, Kingston, ON, Canada. He hold a Ph.D. (2017) and M.Sc. (2012) in Computer Science and Software Engineering from the University of Bradford (UK) and a B.Eng. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of Maiduguri (2008). He was a Research Assistant at the Artificial Intelligence Research Group of the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, of the University of Bradford, UK (2015-2017). His current work is focused on multilevel streaming data analytics with application to Web, media, IoT, and healthcare data processing for predictive analytics. His research interests include: Big Data Analytics and Infrastructure, Streaming Data Analytics, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Graph Analytics.
